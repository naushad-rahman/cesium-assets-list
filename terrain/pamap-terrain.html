Sources of elevation data

Cesium PAMap Terrain currently includes elevation data from the following sources.

Source Coverage Approximate Maximum Spacing Between Height Samples
PAMAP Pennsylvania 1 meter
CGIAR SRTM Approximately -60 to 60 degrees latitude 90 meters
GTOPO30 Entire Earth 1000 meters
