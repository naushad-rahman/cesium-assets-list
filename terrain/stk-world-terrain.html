Sources of elevation data

STK World Terrain currently includes elevation data from the following sources.

Source Coverage Approximate Maximum Spacing Between Height Samples
National Elevation Dataset (NED) United States 10 meters to 30 meters
EU-DEM Europe 30 meters
USGS SRTM 1 Second Global Approximately -60 to 60 degrees latitude 30 meters
CGIAR SRTM Approximately -60 to 60 degrees latitude 90 meters
GTOPO30 Entire Earth 1000 meters
